1. what is the thesis of this essay?

JR Carpenter's article A Handmade Web defines what a handmade website is, telling of its specificity and importance. A handmade website is one that is programmed from scratch by an individual, a web page that is coded by hand rather than by software; a web page that is created and maintained by an individual rather than by a business or a company, and is considered by JR Carpenter to be unique and completely different from the corporate, commercial, and standardized ones. At the same time, JR Carpenter invokes the term "handmade web" to advocate for continued active participation in the production of web pages and web policies.

2. Carpenter describes the handmade web as a form of resistance. In your own words, what is being resisted?

JR Carpenter describes the handmade web as a form of resistance. In your own words, what is being resisted? JR Carpenter describes the handmade web as a form of resistance to corporate, commercialized, and standardized websites, and to exclusive applications that JR Carpenter sees as lacking in individuality, innovation, and uniqueness. JR Carpenter's resistance is to promote and obtain a more diverse and personalized web environment.
