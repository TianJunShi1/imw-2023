2. I prefer skeuo's design concept to flattening. Flattening design is characterized by simplicity, minimalism, and the absence of three-dimensional elements. And the disadvantage that comes with such a feature is that it is very difficult to recognize the characteristics of this kind of software. For example, more and more software nowadays use flat design. When this kind of flat design software floods my phone or computer, it is easy for me not to find the exact software I want to find. But if it's a skeuo design, it's easy to find, because it follows the objects in our display. For example, I can easily find the trash can in my computer.
