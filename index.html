Description of elements

1. Warmth and Security of Home: The house represents the concept of home, implying that animals can obtain the warmth and security of home here, expressing care and concern for pets.

2. Professional treatment and care: Animal footprints symbolize the existence of animals and are associated with the main service recipients of the hospital. At the same time, footprints can also convey information about hospitals providing professional treatment and nursing services.

3. Care and Interaction: Combining the footprints of the house and animals to present a warm scene, reflecting the hospital's care and care for animals, as well as the intimate interaction between owners and pets.
